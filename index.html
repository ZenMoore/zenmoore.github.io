About Me

Welcome! My name is Zekun Moore Wang (Chinese: 王泽坤; French: WANG Noah).

I am a 2nd-year Graduate Student at Beihang University in École Centrale de Pékin (Sino-French Engineering School), working with Dr. Jie Fu, Dr. Wenhao Huang, Wangchunshu Zhou, and Prof. Ke Xu.

My primary research goal is to extend language models to the interactive world scope [1], which I also term InteractiveNLP [2]. The interactivity of LLMs encompasses several dimensions: enhancing user experiences in Human-LM Interaction, their embodiment in the real or virtual environment (Env-LM Interaction), the emergence of an LLM society [3] (LM-LM Interaction), among others (e.g., KB-LM Interaction, Tool-LM Interaction).

To achieve this goal, it involves efforts in two directions: (1) pre-tech: Foundation Models, and (2) post-tech: InteractiveNLP. In (1), I focus on building (pre-training) novel LLMs (esp., Multimodal LLMs) and constructing instruction tuning data [4]. In (2), I am interested in Role-Playing LLM [5], Alignment, Chain of Thought, Retrieval Augmentation, and Agents.
